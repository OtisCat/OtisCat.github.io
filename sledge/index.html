First of all. Sledge is free.

Since Sledge is open source, we are also free. You don't have to deal with licenses, payments, or percentages- just make your content and donate if your happy with what we've made.

Not good enough? We've got community extensions.

Sledge is made by the community, and the best part about us is that we allow community members to build and install any extension they can imagine. This alone allows for more functionality than any other level editing tool, free or payed.

Lastly, We Support Any Game with Custom Levels.

You may need to make a package for it, but Sledge is able to compile any level into any file. Does your game have custom entities? Not a problem, you just describe their attributes, and they'll work. Does your game need custom scripting? No problem, we allow custom scripting, and your package can actually preview the script running too.
